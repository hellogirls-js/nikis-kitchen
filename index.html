How to play

  1. Each player starts off with $200. Use this money to buy ingredients and fill your food queue. You can fit up to ten (10) food items in the food queue.
  2. Click the Serve button to serve an item from the food queue to customers. This will earn you money which you can save for future upgrades, improvements, and plot events.
  3. Be wary of Niki's stomach! If his stomach empties, then you can't serve customers. Use items from the food queue to feed Niki and bring his energy back to keep serving.
